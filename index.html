Share a ride

iShareRyde is an app to help you travel in the comfort of a car, with people you trust and share the cost. Works anywhere in India. There are many people in your known circle of friends, colleagues and neighbours who often travel the same route at roughly the same time e.g. from Home to Office or back. iShareRyde makes it very easy and convenient to reach out to them to share a ride with. It does so through a simple process:

  • Create "Groups" of people who have similar travel requirements as you.
  • When you know what time you'll be leaving, create a ride and invite friends in your Groups.
  • Book a cab from a choice of Service Providers if you are sharing a ride.
  • Pickup friends on the way.

Create clubs and add friends

You can create groups of people who have similar travel requirements as you. Larger the group, greater your chances of finding people. If you don't want to create a group, don't worry - one of your friends could add you to their group and you'll be ready to go.

Create ride and invite people to join the ride

When you know what time you'll be leaving, create a group and invite friends in your Group. You can chose your phone contacts also if you don't have a group. Alternatively, if someone has invited you to share a ride (look out for those notifications), join their ride.

Book a cab, pickup friends on the way

Book a cab from a choice of Service Providers. Pickup friends on the way - Our integration with Google Maps makes it a breeze to identify where they will be. Split the fare at the end of the ride.
